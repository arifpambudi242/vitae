Professional Cover Letter Template

 

INSTRUCTIONS: Purple text indicates the structure of the cover letter. Red text is where you “fill in the blanks”. Green text indicates instructions for you to follow. Blue text indicates advice and suggestions regarding content. Save a copy of this letter as a Master Template. Then rename and save individual versions when you customise it for different applications. Ensure you delete all purple, red, green and blue text before submitting your letter!

 

[Section 1: Formalities, Unique Value Proposition and Introduction]

 

Name of organisation

Address of organisation

 

Date

 

Dear name of contact person or manager associated with the position

 

RE: Application for the role of insert position title and associated reference number here

 

An accomplished detail profession or occupation, I offer list most relevant skill or expertise described in the position description complemented by relevant [industry / professional experience], proven ability to list next most relevant area of expertise or specific outcome described in the position description, and a highly transferrable skill set.

 

Most importantly, I have over number years’ experience [write a broad overview of your professional experience. Refer to your headline statement and the capabilities in your resume].

 

I am now looking to combine this experience with my skills in list broader skills or knowledge, ideally referencing those listed in the position description. As such, I’m seeking [describe the type of role – a new role, progressive role, management or coordinator] roles with organisations that [describe the type of organisation, ideally ensuring that this target organisation fits this criteria – for example, refer to their values statement, projected growth, size of the company, potential opportunities for growth or development. The idea is to show that you want to work for their particular organisation because it matches your career goals].

 

The role of position name with company name offers an ideal opportunity to achieve this goal and take the next step in my career. Please accept this letter, together with my attached resume, in support of my application for the role of position name with company name.

 

[Section 2 : Intent / Motivation. Why you are applying for this particular role / with this particular company]

 

[Choose one of the three following paragraphs, you won’t need all three. Pick the one you feel confident you can add details to]

 

[1] I am familiar with organisation name and I have been following the work you do, particularly in areas such as [outline new projects, developments, cases, clients, customers, store expansions, strategic plans, policies, agendas – this paragraph is to demonstrate that you are genuinely interested in the company and know what they are about]. I have previous experience in / knowledge of  [either provide a broad example or statement of your experience in this particular area, or your knowledge of it, or explain how it is similar to what you have done before]

 

[2] Through my research, I have discovered that your organisation is committed to [research the business and explain why you want to work for them. If you have any knowledge of their business, this is where you demonstrate it. For example, are you familiar with their target audience, stakeholders, clients or specific industry area? Do you know their core business? Are they active in a sector you are familiar with due to a particular role you’ve had in the past?  Are they a large or small organisation?] This appeals to me because [explain the value or opportunities available in a large or small organisation, multinational, industry leader].

 

[3] Since moving on from my previous role I have been actively looking for organisations that I would like to work with, and reached out to speak with person’s name, who [explain how they are related to the company and where they fit within your network, your relationship etc]. They explained  [outline what that contact told you about the organisation you’re interested in working with / potential employer, and the scope of the role and/or opportunities within the organisation]. This greatly appealed to me as it aligns with [explain how this opportunity fits with your career goals / what you are looking for].

 

 [Section 3: Address Specific Employment Barriers]

 

[Only include the following paragraphs if you do not meet all the requirements outlined as essential or required criteria in the job advertisement or position description. Read carefully to select the paragraphs that apply to you, delete all others]

 

[1 Experience] I understand that you are looking for candidates with significant experience in xyz industry area and while I do not have direct experience in this space, I do have relevant experience in related industry xyz. There are many commonalities between these sectors, including [list common areas between where you have worked and where you want to work. Use Google and other job advertisements for inspiration if you need to research this further].

 

I have also had exposure to [list relevant commonalities involving the type of work, the type of client/customer/stakeholder; such as working with a high level of accuracy within stringent regulatory frameworks and leading sales teams in achieving challenging KPIs. As a result, I have experience in [refer to and quote directly from the job advertisement]. Additionally, I possess well-developed transferable skills that will be particularly valuable to this role including [again refer to the position description and select from the capabilities, skills, competencies or attributes sections of your resume].

 

[2 Tertiary Qualifications] I am aware that a tertiary qualification may be considered an asset for this role, however my career journey began (as many of my generation did) when I joined the organisation, company or program as an entry level / VCE graduate / apprentice. I worked my way up within the organisation, and received informal coaching and mentoring in addition to participating in their comprehensive internal training program. I now offer xyz years’ experience in [list area of expertise, occupation type or industry relevant to the job] in lieu of a tertiary qualification. However, over the course of my career I have engaged in numerous training programs and courses, and repeatedly demonstrated my ability to learn new skills and apply theoretical concepts to practical applications. I see from your position description – and from what I know about your organisation – that you will no doubt value this as much as tertiary qualifications.

 

[3 Postgraduate Qualifications] I am aware that a postgraduate qualification may be considered an asset for this role, however the nature of my work has required a more agile approach to learning and professional development, and I have actively sought out mentors to support me in this. I have also participated in comprehensive internal training programs comprised of current, relevant insights and key learnings. This has enriched my ongoing learning considerably. I am, however, open to the prospect of obtaining an appropriate qualification as part of a more formal professional development program in the future.

 

[4 Relocation] While I am currently based in the xyz region, I am aware that the role is based out of city/town/region and I am both willing and able to relocate, at my own cost, if you found me suitable for the position. I am agile, resilient and open to moving for the right role at the right time – and I have the necessary family support to do so.

 

[Section 4: Brief Closing Statement]

 

In summary, I am confident I offer the [detail skills, expertise, experience relevant to the role] necessary to add value to your organisation. This, combined with my [insert personal attributes or soft skills or alignment with organisational values that show you are the right cultural fit for the company] makes me a strong candidate for the position.

 

Thank you for taking the time to consider my application, I look forward to discussing my suitability for this position with you further at your convenience.

 

Yours sincerely,

Andrew
